"Glow" Tropical Smoothie Bowl

A blended base of mango puree, pineapple juice, and sweetened coconut yogurt finished with granola clusters.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened coconut yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • 1/2 cup vanilla granola clusters
  • 2 tablespoons toasted coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. Blend mango, pineapple juice, coconut yogurt, and agave until silky smooth.
  2. Pour into a chilled bowl and tap gently to level the surface.
  3. Top with granola clusters, coconut flakes, and an extra drizzle of agave if desired.
  4. Serve immediately before the toppings soften.

Sugar estimate: ~48g added sugars · Serving size noted: 16 oz bowl

Key elements: Mango puree, pineapple juice, sweetened coconut yogurt, granola clusters

Maple-Glazed Salmon Power Plate

Salmon fillet brushed with maple syrup, plated over brown rice, and topped with a honey-citrus dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet (6 oz)
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 cup steamed broccolini
  • 2 tablespoons honey-citrus vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Brush salmon with maple syrup and sprinkle with brown sugar.
  2. Broil for 8–9 minutes until caramelized and flaky.
  3. Spoon hot rice onto a plate, layer broccolini, then place the salmon on top.
  4. Finish with a generous drizzle of honey-citrus vinaigrette.

Sugar estimate: ~32g added sugars · Serving size noted: Single plated entrée

Key elements: Maple glaze, honey-citrus vinaigrette, brown rice base

Protein-Packed Coffee Shake

Espresso blended with vanilla protein powder, chocolate syrup, ripe banana, and caramel drizzle.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot brewed espresso, cooled
  • 1 frozen ripe banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 1 cup chocolate almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons caramel sauce
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine espresso, banana, protein powder, chocolate syrup, and almond milk in a blender.
  2. Blend on high until frothy and pour into a tall glass.
  3. Swirl caramel sauce along the inside of the glass and over the top.
  4. Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate shavings if serving immediately.

Sugar estimate: ~54g added sugars · Serving size noted: 20 oz shake

Key elements: Chocolate syrup, banana, caramel sauce, flavored protein powder

Sweet Teriyaki "Light" Stir-Fry

Teriyaki-coated vegetables and tofu paired with canned pineapple over jasmine rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice, cooked
  • 1 cup mixed stir-fry vegetables
  • 1 cup firm tofu cubes
  • 1/2 cup canned pineapple tidbits in syrup
  • 1/3 cup bottled teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chili sauce

Instructions

  1. Sauté tofu and vegetables over medium heat until lightly browned.
  2. Stir in pineapple with syrup, teriyaki sauce, and sweet chili sauce.
  3. Simmer 3–4 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the stir-fry.
  4. Spoon over warm jasmine rice and serve immediately.

Sugar estimate: ~40g added sugars · Serving size noted: 2-cup bowl

Key elements: Bottled teriyaki sauce, pineapple syrup, jasmine rice
