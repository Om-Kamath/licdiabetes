Recognize Risky Recipes

Some dishes branded as “diabetes-friendly” still carry a heavy sugar load. Learn the red flags before they derail your progress.

Watch the Toppings

Granola clusters, sweetened yogurt, and syrup drizzles can push sugar content past dessert levels. Always scan the added sugars panel.

Juice ≠ Fiber

Fruit juice bases without pulp or seeds spike blood sugar quickly. Whole fruits blended with leafy greens slow the impact.

Sauces Hide Syrups

Glazes, dressings, or “light” sauces often rely on corn syrup or honey. Ask for dressing on the side and taste before you pour.
